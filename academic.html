Academics Details

Programs Offered

We offer a wide range of academic programs to cater to the diverse needs and interests of our students. Our programs include:

  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Arts (B.A) in English Literature
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Technology(B.Tech) in Mechanical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology(B.Tech) in Civil Engineering
  • Bachelor of Technology(B.Tech) in Electrical Engineering
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Academic Departments

Our college is organized into various academic departments, each specializing in specific fields of study:

  • Department of Civil Engineering
  • Department of Electrical Engineering
  • Department of Commerce
